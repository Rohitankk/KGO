Know Where to Go

If you love to travel by flight, specifically by jets, you've come to the right place. KGO (Know where to go) aims to help you aerophiles by providing information on jet flight information. While flying by jet is a luxury, keeping up to date with prices and other flight information can be a hassle. It isn't also always the easiest task to determine whether jet flights are available to a destination. KGO solves these problems for you by searching for airports within an 800 km radius of your location and a specified destination anywhere around the world. Among other useful information, KGO provides information on rates and top speeds of jets of varying types making it easier for you to determine what jet to book next. So whether you're a jet flight connoisseur or dreaming to get in a jet on your next flight, KGO will help you get ready to embark on your unforgettable journeys, one takeoff at a time.
